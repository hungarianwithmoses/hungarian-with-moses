Your Teacher

Hi, I'm Moses

I'm a native Hungarian speaker, a professional tutor, and a polyglot — I speak Hungarian, English, Spanish, Italian, Romanian, and Turkish. Through those lessons I built my own method for getting people speaking real Hungarian fast, without drowning in grammar tables first. This course is that method, distilled into 46 videos you can work through at your own pace.
